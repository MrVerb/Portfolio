COMPANY

Horizon NY Inc. ("HNY") is a privately held company with over 25 years of experience in the import as well as export of apparel. We design, manufacture and import/export young mens, juniors and children's wear for numerous makor retailers throughout North America and Europe. Horizon has a commitment to providing contemporary fashion and value across all key soft line categories and age groups.

Long standing relationships with all of North America's top retailers including Macy's, JCPenney, Wal-Mart, Target, Sears, K-mart and various specialty stores, and a strong understanding of industry trends, are part of the foundation that has enabled HNY to become a well-respected industry leader in apparel.

BRANDS/LICENSE

HNY is a recognized industry leader in apparel known for cutting edge design and a keen understanding of popular culture. This enables our company to keep our accounts successful and on top of the latest trends.

  • HORIZON - successful young mens, jrs and boys lines currently being sold in department stores such as Macy's and Dillards as well as mass and mid-tier accounts including Wal-mart, Target, JCPenney, and K-mart.
  • TRES CHIC - Juniors collection focusing mainly on the specialty market including Charlotte Russe, Mandee's, Windsor Stores, and Le Chateau.
  • MONGOOSE - The industry leader in BMX bicycles for over 40 years, Mongoose is an aggressive brand with products that epitomize the look and attitude of the action sports consumer. Focusing on licensed denim and apparel, this line is being sold in accounts nationwide including K-mart and Sears.
